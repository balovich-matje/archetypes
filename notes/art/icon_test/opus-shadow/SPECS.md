# The Shadow — node icons

Every icon is built from a real vanilla sprite pulled out of the 26.2 client
jar (recoloured / cropped / composed at 16px so pixels land on the game's own
grid), then upscaled 2x NEAREST to the 32px node canvas — the house norm. On
each sprite sits one small readable cue. A shared grammar runs across the tree:
a **crescent moon** = the dark (the tree's own symbol), a **heart** = healing,
**speed-dashes** = faster, a **glow outline** = "it lights up", a **fade** =
"it vanishes", a **plus** = "more", a **slash** = "removed / cancelled", a
**surge-arrow** = "spiked", **orange eye-glow** = the tree's predatory stare
(the same glare the Invisibility active wears), and **gold corner-brackets** =
a capstone / the crown.

Skips INVISIBILITY (its icon exists) and MINOR.

## Outer arc — surviving the dark

- **night_eyes** — The vanilla Night Vision effect icon, which *is* a crescent
  moon on a starry sky (the tree's symbol), with two orange eyes waking in the
  dark below it. Reads: Night Vision, literally, plus "eyes".
- **swift_shadow** — Sugar (vanilla's Speed source) with three white
  speed-dashes streaming off it. Reads: move faster.
- **dark_mending** — A glistering melon slice (vanilla's regen reagent) with a
  heart lifting off it under a small crescent. Reads: hearts regrow while hidden.
- **dim_presence** — A phantom membrane thinned almost to nothing (you fade from
  the world) behind an eye struck through with red. Reads: they look and see no one.
- **cleansing_veil** — A milk bucket (the one thing in vanilla that wipes every
  effect) with a poison mote slashed out beside it. Reads: casting scrubs your ailments.
- **last_shadow** *(capstone)* — The Totem of Undying (vanilla's cheat-death)
  trailing up into shadow-smoke as it fires you invisible; gold corner-frame. Reads:
  cheat death and vanish.

## Inner arc — killing in it

- **stillness** — A gold clock with a white plus. Reads: the vanish timer runs longer.
- **first_strike** — The iron sword lunging out of a dark echo of itself, a white
  impact star bursting at its point. Reads: the strike from nowhere hits harder.
- **bloodrush** — A pile of redstone (vanilla's Strength reagent, blood-red for
  the rush) with a bright surge-arrow spiking off it. Reads: a kill spikes your power.
- **reaper** — A wither rose (the black bloom death leaves where a mob fell), its
  withering wisps curling up into a bright heart. Reads: the kill hands you life.
- **umbral_sight** — A creeper's face (the game's most-known "hostile") wrapped in
  the cyan outline vanilla paints on Glowing mobs. Reads: enemies light up for you.
- **ghost_armor** — A chainmail chestplate dissolving left-to-right into a few
  stray sparkles. Reads: the armour goes invisible with its wearer.
- **predator** *(capstone)* — A skull with its eye-sockets burning the tree's
  hunting orange; gold corner-frame. Reads: the apex of the dark that never leaves it.

## The crown

- **umbral_mastery** — The Eye of Ender turned all-seeing, haloed in shadow-light,
  full gold-and-white frame — brightest and most ornate. Reads: the master's eye,
  the crown of the constellation. (Effect is a placeholder in-game; the icon just
  says "the top".)
