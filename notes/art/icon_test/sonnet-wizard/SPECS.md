# Wizard node icons — specs

One 32x32 icon per family (MINOR skipped), built by `make.py` from vanilla
sprites pulled out of the client jar plus a mod texture (the mana orb) and a
handful of hand-plotted effect pixels. Order below matches the tree: haft,
grip, diamond, crown.

**magic_missile** — the mod's own wand low-left, an amethyst shard already
clear of the tip up in the top-right corner, three fading sparkle pixels
strung between them. Reads as a shot just fired, not a static wand.

**mana_shield** — the vanilla HUD heart, left half its usual red, right half
recoloured to mana blue (same palette as the mana orb). A heart that's half
mana *is* the mechanic — half your damage skips your health bar.

**force** — the breeze rod with a hot red/gold burst at its tip (deliberately
warmer than the missile's cool purple, so it doesn't read as the same spell)
and a plain white '+' in the free corner for the flat, stacking damage add.

**clarity** — the mana orb with a flat red '-' in the same corner spot
Arcane Orb puts its '+' — same grammar, opposite sign: this shrinks the
*cost*, not the pool.

**siphon** — a glass bottle with mana-blue liquid traced from the bottle's
own silhouette (so it never leaks past the glass) pooled at the base, plus a
kill-spark above with a drip strung down into the neck.

**echo** — two amethyst shards on the same line, one solid, one faded —
the tree's established "ghost twin" motif for a free duplicate cast.

**range** — the spyglass with a short dashed sightline trailing off the
lens past the canvas edge: farther sight standing in for farther reach.

**arcane_orb** — the mana orb blown up to fill most of the frame with a
plain white '+' beside it: the flat, no-frills capacity add.

**velocity** — the feather with three speed-line dashes streaming off its
trailing edge.

**overwhelm** — the vanilla half-heart (real HP already spent) with a
couple of gold/red damage flecks at the broken edge: bonus damage against a
target that's already bleeding.

**concussion** — vanilla's own Weakness effect icon, straight off the
status HUD, with a couple of amethyst flecks tying the debuff back to a
missile hit.

**shatterpoint** — the archery target block (pristine, rings unbroken) with
a bold dark-outlined white crack punched straight through the centre where
the hit lands: bonus damage against a target that *isn't* wounded yet, the
deliberate mirror of Overwhelm.

**seeker_missile** — the Eye of Ender (vanilla's own "seeks the stronghold"
item) with a bent dashed line curving after it: the missile steering itself
in.

**lance** — the vanilla trident, the one polearm already drawn as a proper
diagonal spear, with three small pierce-pips down its shaft for the
multiple enemies it runs through.

**mind_well** — lapis lazuli with a climbing tally of small blue motes
leading up to a bright four-point spark: the count building toward every
8th (then 4th) empowered missile.

**flow** — the Heart of the Sea with a column of small upward chevrons
rising off it in mana blue: a steady tide, not a one-off sparkle.

**archmage** — the nether star almost as vanilla ships it (the tree's own
capstone shine needs no help) with two small amethyst motes worked into the
points so it still reads as *this* tree's capstone.
