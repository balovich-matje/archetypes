# Assassin node icons — specs

One 32x32 icon per family (16px vanilla art composed on the vanilla grid, saved
2x NEAREST). Every icon is anchored on a **real sprite** — a vanilla item/block/
effect pulled from the client jar, or the mod's own dagger textures — with a
small mechanic grammar hand-plotted on top. Grammar used across the sheet:
`+` = more · ghost copies = a strike duplicated · speed dashes = faster ·
coloured motes/drip = an applied effect · cracked/cut = defence undone ·
refresh loop = a cooldown reset · portal sparks = Shadow Step's blink.

## The active + its improvers
- **shadow_step** — ender_pearl (teleport) with the mod iron dagger driven through it, purple portal sparks around. Reads *blink in, stab*: the exact active.
- **adrenaline_rush** — sugar (vanilla's swiftness reagent) kicking upward in speed chevrons, one portal spark tying it to the blink. Reads *Speed burst after Shadow Step*, and the boot-less anchor keeps it distinct from Lightfoot.
- **opportunist** — the golden clock with a backward rewind arrow on its shoulder. Clock = cooldown; the rewind = the ability comes *sooner*.

## The body work (footwork)
- **lightfoot** — leather boots with speed dashes streaming off the heels. The boots are the anchor, the dashes say *faster on your feet*.
- **sidestep** — a feather (nimbleness) with a red blow whiffing past above it and a white dodge swoosh curving away below. The missed strike + the evade arc read *dodge the melee entirely*.

## Left edge — raw steel
- **razor_edge** — the mod iron dagger with a bright honed-edge glint, a point spark and a gold `+`. Plain dagger + more = *more dagger damage*.
- **expose** — vanilla's target bullseye with a half-emptied heart in front. Bullseye = the mark; the half heart = *only once they're below half health*.

## Right edge — what's on the steel
- **venom** — a spider eye leaking bright green poison bubbles and a drip. Green venom on the dagger's reagent = *hits apply Poison*.
- **blight** — the wither rose beside vanilla's own Wither effect icon (the blackened heart it inflicts), pale decay wisps between them. Rose + the real Wither icon = *hits apply Wither*.
- **flense** — vanilla shears biting an iron chestplate, the plate split by a bright cut. Cutting the armour open = *damage ignores armour*.

## Capstones + the point
- **shadow_flurry** — the mod netherite dagger with two ghost echoes fanned behind it: three blades at once, the x3.0 weight of the flurry.
- **momentum** — a wither-bone skull crowned by a green refresh loop. Skull = a kill; the green loop = *Shadow Step winds back to ready*.
- **deathblow** — a nether star (the point of the tree) with the dagger struck across it, an impact flare and a gold `+`. The star marks the ultimate; dagger + more = *every Shadow Step strike lands harder*.
