# Slayer node icons — round three

The Slayer is swords + two-handed greatswords, so every icon is built on the
actual weapon it modifies: the vanilla `item/iron_sword` for sword-path and
shared families, the mod's own `item/iron_greatsword` for greatsword-path
families. On top of the real sprite sits the settled mechanic grammar — `+`/gold
= more, ghost copy = an extra strike, speed dashes = faster/movement,
down-chevrons = slowed, blood drip = bleed/lifesteal, refresh loop = cooldown
reset. Weapon match follows the tree: sword-path (Lunge, Rend, Blade Dance,
Flurry, Bladestorm) shows the sword; greatsword-path (Immovable, Heavy Blows,
First Blood, Executioner, Decimate) shows the greatsword.

Every icon: 16px art upscaled 2x NEAREST, except the greatsword ones already
composed at their native 32px.

- **Hamstring** (`slowness.png`) — iron_sword + cold-blue down-chevrons stacked where it struck: the blade applies Slowness.
- **Taste of Blood** (`taste_of_blood.png`) — vanilla full heart drinking a rising blood drip, faded blade behind: kills heal you.
- **Lunge** (`lunge.png`) — iron_sword thrusting up-right, speed dashes trailing off the grip: a sprinting swing flings you forward and up.
- **Immovable** (`kbres.png`) — greatsword over an obsidian block, Minecraft's own byword for won't-be-moved: knockback resistance.
- **Rend** (`bleed.png`) — two open sword-gashes with blood running off the bottom: a cut that bleeds over time.
- **Blade Dance** (`blade_dance.png`) — the real blade up-right and a ghost copy striking up-left, arcs off both tips: one strike, a second enemy hit any direction.
- **Heavy Blows** (`heavy.png`) — greatsword with a gold `+` off the tip (more) and slow down-chevrons off the grip (slower): hits harder, swings slower.
- **First Blood** (`firstblood.png`) — clean greatsword, its tip drawing the first drop from a full heart: bonus vs an unhurt target.
- **Flurry** (`flurry.png`) — iron_sword wrapped in a green refresh loop: sword kills reset Lunge's cooldown.
- **Executioner** (`executioner.png`) — greatsword tip come down onto a bare skeleton skull: finishes any low-health target outright.
- **Bloodlust** (`bloodlust.png`) — a fat blood drop driven fast, speed dashes streaking off it, splatter flung behind: kills grant Speed.
- **Relentless** (`relentless.png`) — the vanilla clock wound faster, speed dashes running off it: your capstone comes back sooner.
- **Bladestorm** (`bladestorm.png`) — capstone: a fan of the same sword sweeping one way mid-whirl, sweep sparks on the leading arc.
- **Decimate** (`decimate.png`) — capstone: vanilla's own sweep flash with the greatsword driven through it — one huge tilted cleave.
