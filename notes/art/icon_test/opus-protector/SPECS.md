# Protector node icons — opus-protector

Sword-and-board tank. Every icon starts from a real vanilla sprite: the wooden
shield front-plate (the flat pixels of `Items.SHIELD`, cut from
`entity/shield/shield_base_nopattern.png`, with one hand-plotted iron boss so it
reads as a shield not a plank) is the recurring hero, matching what the tree
draws under most Protector nodes in-game. Vanilla `strength`, `clock`, `arrow`,
`wind_charge` and the `resistance` blue-shield are composed in where they already
carry the mechanic. Grammar layered on top: `+`=more, ghost copies=omni/duplicate,
speed dashes=faster/lunge, arc=wide, launched target=knockback, clock=cooldown,
spikes=thorns, bounced arrow=reflect, green bar=durability kept, cracked ground=slam.

- **bash** (Shield Bash) — shield thrust right, motion streaks trailing, a big impact bursting off its face. The active shove.
- **slam** (Shield Slam) — vanilla Strength sprite (the game's "+attack damage" mark) rising behind the shield, with a bold `+`: the bash hits harder.
- **cooldown** (Quick Recovery) — vanilla clock plus a green down-arrow: the bash's cooldown drops.
- **knockback** (Concussive Blow) — the shield launches a red target clean off the right edge behind a bold arrow: trade damage for a huge shove.
- **wide** (Wide Swings) — a broad sweep arc thrown over the shield, three targets struck along it: the bash hits everyone in front.
- **unbreaking** (Reinforced Straps) — an iron band rivets the plate and a full green durability bar rides underneath: the shield keeps its durability.
- **spikes** (Iron Spikes) — iron spikes jut from every edge of the raised shield, one tip bloodied: Thorns on block.
- **rush** (Shield Rush) — the shield charges, a wind-charge puff at its back and long speed streaks streaming off it: the blocking lunge.
- **braced** (Braced) — a hit sparks off the raised shield while a corner clock ticks down: blocked hits shave the cooldown.
- **reflect** (Reflection) — an arrow pings off the shield's face and ricochets back up-and-away: blocked projectiles return.
- **taunt** (Taunt) — a red `!` over the shield with enemies converging from all four corners: every monster turns to attack you.
- **omni_block** (Bulwark, capstone) — the shield flanked by two warded blue ghost shields, a resistance-blue aura ringing the whole guard: block from every direction.
- **ground_slam** (Ground Slam, capstone) — the shield driven down, the ground cracking open and a shockwave rolling out both sides: the bash becomes an AoE.
