# Priest node icons — specs

**The visual grammar.** One shared motif per meaning, so a family's *domain*
reads before its badge does:

- **Gold white-cored orb** = Holy Light itself (holy_light, lumen, radiance, fervent_cast, ascendant)
- **Blue orb** = mana math (grace, devotion, beacon)
- **Red heart** = healing the living (mercy, renewal)
- **Bone skull** = harming the undead (wrath, immolation, judgement)
- **Gold absorption heart** = protective shell (aegis, sanctuary)
- **Golden apple** = a granted blessing (benediction)

A secondary badge (plus / minus / arrow / rings / flame) carries the exact
mechanic. Vanilla sprites give the palette (hearts, effects, flame, apple);
the orb, skull, shield and arrows are hand-plotted in matching colors.

## Icons

- **holy_light** — gold sunburst orb with a faint lob-trail arcing in from below. The active: a thrown burst of holy light.
- **lumen** — the orb plus a bold "+". Raw power scaling both the heal and the smite.
- **grace** — blue mana orb stamped with a big minus and a down-chevron. Costs less mana.
- **radiance** — a cool sea-lantern-white orb throwing expanding concentric rings. Light reaching further.
- **devotion** — mana orb with two green arrows flowing up. Mana regenerates over time.
- **fervent_cast** — orb streaking right on hard horizontal speed-lines with a forward chevron. Flies faster and flatter.
- **mercy** — full red heart with a bold "+". More healing, the mercy arm only.
- **wrath** — bone skull with a bold red "+". More damage to undead, the wrath arm only.
- **renewal** — the vanilla Regeneration heart ringed by a gold halo, lifted on pink sparkles. Capstone: grants Regeneration to allies.
- **benediction** — golden apple flinging four differently-colored buff sparks. Capstone: a random blessing (speed/strength/resist/fire-res).
- **beacon** — a large mana orb pressed into a container ring, with a "+". More *max* mana (capacity, not rate).
- **immolation** — skull with flame rising off the crown. Sets undead ablaze.
- **aegis** — a shield bearing a gold absorption heart. Shells *you* in absorption.
- **sanctuary** — a gold protective dome arcing over two gold absorption hearts. Shells *allies* in absorption.
- **judgement** — skull sapped by two sickly-green down-arrows. Lays Weakness on undead.
- **ascendant** — the Holy Light orb crowned by a full halo, long rays and an ascending chevron. Top capstone: everything 25% stronger.

## Notes
- Three blue-orb nodes are the subtlest to tell apart; they're separated by badge only: grace = minus (down), devotion = up-arrows (rate), beacon = ring + plus (capacity).
- The two crossbar arms deliberately mirror: mercy (heart + plus) vs wrath (skull + plus).
- The three skull nodes share the hand-plotted skull and differ only by badge: plus (wrath), flame (immolation), down-arrows (judgement).
